SYNTHESIS OF SILICA IN RAW RICE HUSK

R.KALYAN

AIM and MATERIAL REQUIREMENT

The synthesis of silica from rice husk involves extracting and processing the silica content present in the husk.Rice husk is a rich source of silica, and the process typically involves solgel techniques. Material requirement: Rice husk (10 gram),HCL,NaoH.

PROCEDURE

Take raw husk weight 10gm

Prepare 1:5M HCl

Acid leaching the husk powder at 1:5 ratio

Magnetic stirring for 1.5 hrs for 750 rpm

Centrifuge at 6000 rpm for 5mins

Muffle for 850 c for 2hrs

Weight the final powder

ALKALI PROCESS

5M NaoH mix with muffeled powder 1:3 ratio

Centrifuge the mixture for 1.5 hrs at 750rpm

Discard the precipitate

Keep supernanant and titrate against HCl 0.6M

Gel formation

Centrifuge for 8000rpm for 5mins

Take gel

Muffled the gel for 850c for 2hrs

Obtained silica nanoparticle
